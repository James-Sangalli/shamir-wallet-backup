Shamir Secret Sharing Scheme for Crypto

Split your seed phrase into parts which can be combined back into the original secret using some or all of the parts.

This is a simple POC with the idea in mind that one day you should be able to split your secret phrase amongst multiple cloud providers, retrieve your backup without relying on individuals and prevent an attacker from gaining access to all your shares.

This tool is experimental, use it at your own risk!

Steps

1. Input your seed/private key or use the one auto generated.

2. Select the number of shares required to reconstruct your key.

3. Verify that you are able to reconstruct the key by using the combine box.

4. Split the shares among dropbox and google drive or another provider. Make sure that it requires access to more than one account to reconstruct.

5. Download the page to your computer so you have a full backup offline.
